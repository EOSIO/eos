template<typename BaseToken , typename QuoteToken >
const uint64_t eos::price< BaseToken, QuoteToken >::precision = 1000ll*1000ll*1000ll*1000ll*1000ll
static

The largest base 10 integer that can be represented with 53 bits of a double. This number keeps the math in range of JavaScript. By being a power of 10 it makes it easier for developers to read and interpret the integer by simply shifting the decimal.
